## Deploy a LAMP stack using Cloud Marketplace

In the Google Cloud console, in the <b>Navigation menu</b> (Navigation menu icon), click ``Marketplace``.

<img width="287" height="605" alt="lampstack-1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8832a340-e457-4cfa-a949-57c32484189d" />
<br>
<br>

In the search bar, type ```LAMP``` and then press ``ENTER``. In the search results, click ``LAMP Stack``, by ``Google Click to Deploy``.

<img width="1033" height="451" alt="lampstack-2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c8bad0df-ee28-45ee-9b28-d032aea5f20a" />
<br>
<br>


On the LAMP page, click ``GET STARTED``.

<img width="647" height="249" alt="lampstack-3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d337f903-14ec-42c1-9044-b3f094ee0278" />
<br>
<br>

On the Agreements page, check the box for Terms and agreements, and click ``AGREE``.

<img width="637" height="705" alt="lampstack-4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dbf587a9-b1b1-4054-8c13-d849dab699a5" />
<br>
<br>

On the Successfully agreed to terms pop up, click ``DEPLOY``.

<img width="559" height="309" alt="lampstack-5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bcf304d4-7b63-42a0-b198-23965ca8f745" />
<br>
<br>


If prompted, click on ``Enable`` for the <b>Compute Engine API</b> and the <b>Infrastructure Manager API</b>.

<img width="510" height="263" alt="lampstack-6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fef891d8-22e3-49dd-92cc-d2d87ae64ff2" />
<br>
<br>

For <b>Zone</b>, select the deployment zone to ``us-central1-c``. For <b>Machine Type</b>, select ``E2`` as the Series and ``e2-medium`` as the <b>Machine Type</b>.

<img width="508" height="835" alt="lampstack-7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/094a4e25-f667-42c6-81c0-831075c351ac" />
<br>
<br>

Leave the remaining settings as their defaults then click ``Deploy``.

<img width="418" height="222" alt="lampstack-8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dbdc08f0-2cdb-48d7-a0de-69f481739ccd" />
<br>
